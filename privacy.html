Privacy

Website analytics

See the Pattern uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand general website traffic and performance. Cloudflare Web Analytics does not use cookies or local storage and is designed not to collect visitors’ personal data.

The website also records anonymous aggregate counts of selected interactions, such as opening a reference list, following a reference, watching a video, selecting a support option, or opening the contact page.

The interaction counter does not use cookies, local storage, visitor identifiers or browser fingerprinting. It does not store names, email addresses, IP addresses or an individual visitor’s browsing history.

Only limited information needed to understand the interaction is stored, such as the type of link selected, the related video identifier, platform, location on the website, reference category and the date. Results are stored as combined daily totals rather than individual visitor records.

This information is processed using Cloudflare Workers and Cloudflare D1 and is used only to understand how the website is being used and to improve its content and navigation.

Contact

Questions about this privacy information can be sent through the contact details provided on the website.
